# Cinch IT — AI Command Center Demo Script

## Opening (30 seconds)

> "J, imagine waking up every morning and instead of checking 4 different tools, putting out fires, wondering which clients are about to churn — you open ONE screen and your AI has already done the work for you."

*Open index.html in browser. Let it load — the animations will do the talking for a beat.*

> "This is what an AI operating system looks like for an IT services company."

## The KPIs (1 min)

Point to the top row of cards.

> "Right here — your entire business health in 5 seconds. Revenue, clients, tickets, resolution time, MRR growth. No logging into ConnectWise, no spreadsheets. It's pulled, calculated, and trended automatically."

**Key detail:** Point out the trend arrows/percentages. "These aren't static — the AI is comparing to last month and flagging what needs attention."

## Morning War Room (2 min)

> "This is the killer feature. Every morning at 6 AM, your AI runs a war room. It looks at everything — tickets, SLAs, contracts up for renewal, tech utilization — and gives you a prioritized battle plan."

Walk through each item:
- **Critical alerts** (red) — "These are fires. SLA breach risk, server down, whatever. You see these first."
- **Today's priorities** — "AI ranked these by business impact, not just urgency."
- **Action items** — "These are specific things to do today. Not a to-do list you wrote — the AI generated this from your data."

> "You're not reacting anymore. You're operating."

## Client Health (1-2 min)

> "Every client scored automatically. Green means happy and profitable. Yellow means something's off — maybe ticket volume spiked, maybe they haven't renewed yet. Red means you're about to lose them."

Point to a yellow/red client:
> "See Meridian Legal? Ticket volume up 34%, satisfaction score dropping. Without this, you find out when they cancel. With this, you call them TODAY and save the account."

**Pain point to hit:** "How much revenue have you lost to clients who churned that you didn't see coming?"

## Revenue Pipeline (1 min)

> "Your revenue isn't just a number — it's broken down. Recurring vs. project vs. expansion. You can see exactly where growth is coming from and where it's stalling."

> "And look — contract renewals coming up. The AI flags which ones are at risk so you can get ahead of it."

## Team Productivity (1 min)

> "Your techs are your biggest cost. Are they utilized? Are tickets balanced? Who's overloaded, who's underutilized?"

> "Right now you probably figure this out by gut feel or asking around. The AI just shows you."

## AI Insights (2 min — this is the closer)

> "This is where it gets wild. The AI doesn't just show you data — it thinks about your business."

Read a few insights aloud:
- "Based on ticket trends, recommend adding a Level 2 tech for Q2"
- "3 clients showing pre-churn patterns — proactive outreach recommended"
- "Tuesday ticket volume 40% above baseline — consider staggering tech schedules"

> "This is like having a COO who never sleeps, never forgets, and gets smarter every week."

## Voice Demo Flow (Optional — if Jamie wants to freestyle)

Pretend to talk to the AI:
> **Jamie:** "What's my biggest risk this week?"
> **AI response:** "Meridian Legal Group has had 3 escalated tickets in 5 days with declining satisfaction. They're up for renewal in 45 days. Recommend scheduling a QBR this week."

> **Jamie:** "What about revenue?"
> **AI:** "MRR is up 4.2% but 2 contracts totaling $8,400/mo renew next month with below-average health scores. Net risk: potential -$16,800 ARR if both churn."

## The Close

> "J, this isn't science fiction. This is built on the same AI stack I work with every day. The question isn't whether this technology exists — it's whether Cinch IT is going to use it before your competitors do."

> "I can build this for your actual data. ConnectWise, Autotask, whatever you're on. Real numbers, real insights, running every morning before you wake up."

**Let him react. Don't oversell. The demo speaks for itself.**

---

## Key Pain Points to Weave In

1. **Client churn blindness** — "You find out too late"
2. **Ticket overload / firefighting** — "You're always reacting"
3. **Revenue visibility** — "Do you know your MRR growth rate right now?"
4. **Tech utilization** — "Your biggest cost, managed by gut feel"
5. **Scaling without hiring** — "AI lets you run a 50-person operation with 15 people"

## Objection Handling

- **"This looks expensive"** → "What does losing one $5K/mo client cost you? This pays for itself with one save."
- **"We use ConnectWise already"** → "This sits ON TOP of ConnectWise. It makes your existing tools 10x more useful."
- **"I don't have time to set this up"** → "I set it up. You just open a browser."
- **"Is this real?"** → "The AI and integrations are real. This specific dashboard is a mockup to show you the vision. Building it on your real data is step 2."
