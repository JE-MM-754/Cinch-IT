## The Core Model: "Sales Android" / ROYA System

### The Premise
Every business has dead leads sitting in their database — leads they spent real money acquiring that went nowhere. These leads are:
- Already familiar with the business
- Already had some level of intent
- Sitting in a CRM collecting dust
- Written off as worthless by the sales team

**Wardrope's insight:** These leads aren't dead — they're just dormant. With the right re-engagement approach (AI + SMS), 3-7% can be reactivated into paying customers.

### The 3-Step Process

**Step 1: The List**
- Business hands over their "dead" lead database (contacts that sales gave up on)
- Minimum viable list: 500+ contacts with phone numbers
- Best leads: 6-24 months dormant (still remember the business, still need the service)

**Step 2: The Sales Android**
- AI-powered SMS bot sends a short, conversational text to each lead
- The key: **2-sentence SMS** — not long pitches. Feels like a real person checking in.
- Example: "Hey [Name], this is [Rep] from [Business]. We helped you with [service] a while back — are you still looking for help with that?"
- AI handles the back-and-forth conversation, qualifies interest, handles objections
- Positive responses get sent a booking calendar link
- **Tool stack:** GoHighLevel (GHL), CloseBot, or custom n8n/Twilio setup
- A2P verified SMS (10DLC registration required for business texting)

**Step 3: The Profit**
- Qualified leads book calls directly on the business's calendar
- Business closes the sales using their existing team
- **Revenue model:** Performance-based only
  - Per qualified appointment ($50-200 per sat appointment)
  - Or revenue share (25-50% of closed revenue)
  - Or hybrid (small setup fee + per appointment)
- **Key selling point:** "You only pay when you make money. Zero risk."

### Revenue Model Details (from book + community)
- **Wardrope's pricing:** 25% of increased revenue (agency keeps 75% since they do the work)
- **Community variation:** 30-50% rev share is common for smaller businesses
- **Per appointment:** $50-200 per qualified, sat appointment
- **Campaign scale:** $100K+ campaigns, keeping $30-50K for yourself
- **Case studies:**
  - Roofing company: $53K from 773 dead leads
  - Gary (case study): Sale in 3 hours from a list of just 25 contacts
  - UK business: £287K from abandoned leads

### Why It Works
1. **SMS has 98% open rate** vs 20% for email
2. **Conversational AI** feels personal, not spammy
3. **Leads already know the business** — trust barrier is lower
4. **Zero acquisition cost** — the business already paid for these leads
5. **Performance-based = easy sell** — business has nothing to lose

### Critical Adjustment: Business Landlines vs. Mobile

**Problem:** Only 29 contacts have mobile phone numbers. The Wardrope model relies heavily on SMS to mobile phones. Most Cinch IT contacts have business landlines (508, 617, 774 area codes).

**Solutions:**
1. **Text-enabled landlines** — Many businesses now have VoIP systems that receive texts. Worth testing.
2. **Email-first approach** — For the 325 contacts with email, lead with AI email outreach
3. **Phone + email combo** — AI email to warm up, then phone follow-up for non-responders
4. **AI voice call** — Use AI voice agents (Bland.ai, Vapi, etc.) to call business landlines instead of texting
5. **Data enrichment** — Run company names through Apollo.io or ZoomInfo to find mobile numbers and updated contacts

### Recommended Hybrid Approach for Cinch IT

Instead of pure SMS (Wardrope's default), use a **3-channel Sales Android:**

1. **Channel 1: AI Email** (325 contacts with email)
   - Short, conversational email from "Jack Wilson" or the Cinch IT team
   - "Hey [Name], we helped [Company] with IT a while back. Are you still looking for managed IT support?"
   - AI handles replies, qualifies, books meetings

2. **Channel 2: AI SMS** (29 mobile numbers + test text-enabled landlines)
   - Classic Wardrope 2-sentence SMS
   - Only for confirmed mobile/text-capable numbers
   - A2P/10DLC registration required

3. **Channel 3: AI Voice** (607 phone numbers — the full database)
   - AI voice agent calls business numbers
   - "Hi, this is [name] calling from Cinch IT. We worked with [Company] a while back and wanted to check in..."
   - Tools: Bland.ai, Vapi, Retell.ai
   - Best for B2B where people answer office phones

### Data Cleaning Required Before Launch

1. **Remove junk contacts:** "admin, admin" entries, 999-999-9999 phones, na@na.com emails
2. **Deduplicate:** Multiple contacts at same company — pick the best one
3. **Enrich:** Run company names through Apollo.io to get:
   - Updated contact names/titles
   - Mobile phone numbers
   - Current email addresses
   - Company size/industry
   - Whether business is still operating
4. **Segment:** Tag by industry (legal, medical, manufacturing, etc.) for personalized messaging
5. **Verify:** Check which businesses are still active (Google Maps, website check)
