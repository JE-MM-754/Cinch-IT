5. POC next steps need to be explicit and structured at end of every call
6. Pricing scalar too complex — simplify for faster deal velocity
7. Control plane vs compute plane — even the customer was confused. Need simple language.
8. No TrueFoundry SDK requirement = vendor lock-in prevention — massive, undersold differentiator
